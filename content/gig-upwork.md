## Overview

I build AI-powered WhatsApp automation systems for small and medium businesses. My focus: appointment booking, client communication, and workflow automation that saves real time and real money.

**What I specialize in:**

WhatsApp Business API integrations with intelligent conversation flows. I don't build generic chatbots — I build systems that handle the full appointment lifecycle: booking, confirmation, rescheduling, cancellation, and 24-hour reminders. All automated. All on WhatsApp, where your clients already are.

**My stack:**
n8n (workflow automation), WhatsApp Business API (via Meta Cloud API or providers like WATI/Twilio), Google Sheets / Notion / Airtable for calendar management, and OpenAI API when the use case calls for natural language understanding. I can work with your existing tools or recommend the right stack for your budget.

**Real work, real clients:**

I built the full WhatsApp appointment system for GLAM NAILS, a nail salon in Heredia, Costa Rica. Their clients now book, reschedule, and cancel directly on WhatsApp — 24/7 — without any staff involvement. The system handles multiple concurrent conversations, enforces rate limits to block spam, forwards reference images clients send (nail designs, for example) to the business owner, and logs every conversation for review.

Before this system, they were losing bookings every night and weekend. Now they don't.

**Types of projects I take on:**

— WhatsApp chatbots for salons, clinics, spas, veterinaries, gyms, and any service business that runs on appointments
— n8n workflow automation (multi-step automations connecting APIs, databases, messaging platforms)
— WhatsApp Business API setup and configuration (I handle the Meta approval process)
— AI-assisted customer support flows using OpenAI + WhatsApp
— Zapier/Make to n8n migrations for teams that need more control and lower per-task costs

**How I work:**

I take on a limited number of projects at a time so I can be responsive and do the work right. When you hire me, you get direct communication, clear timelines, and a working system at the end — not a prototype.

For fixed-price projects: I scope the work before we start, agree on deliverables in writing, and deliver with a testing period included.

For hourly: I log hours accurately, update you daily, and flag scope changes before acting on them.

**Not a fit for:** Large enterprise systems, mobile app development, or projects that need a team. I'm a solo specialist — I go deep, not wide.

If you have a business that runs on appointments and your WhatsApp is a mess of unread messages, I can fix that. Let's talk.
