## Portfolio — GLAM NAILS Case Study

**Client:** GLAM NAILS — Nail salon, Heredia, Costa Rica
**Owner:** Karolina Madrigal

**The problem:** The salon was receiving booking requests on WhatsApp at all hours. When no one was available to respond, clients moved on. Evenings and weekends were the worst — high intent, zero coverage.

**What I built:** A full WhatsApp appointment bot that handles the entire booking flow without human involvement. Clients message the salon's WhatsApp number, select their service from a menu, choose a date and time, and receive an instant confirmation. The bot also handles rescheduling and cancellation requests, sends a reminder 24 hours before each appointment, and forwards reference images (nail designs clients send) directly to the owner.

The system includes rate limiting to block spam conversations, and every conversation is logged so the owner can review interactions at any time.

**Stack:** n8n + WhatsApp Business API (Meta Cloud API) + Google Sheets

**Result:** The salon now takes bookings 24/7 with zero manual work. No missed messages, no lost clients during off-hours.

**Delivery time:** 1.5 hours setup, live the same day.
